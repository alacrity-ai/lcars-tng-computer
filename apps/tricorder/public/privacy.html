Privacy

Last updated: July 2026. Short version: this service exists to move your words from your phone to your own computer. We keep as little as possible for as short as possible.

What we store

  • Account data — your email, a password hash (never the password), household name, and member handles.
  • Commands in flight — a voice command is held only until your home computer confirms receipt, and is discarded unread after 60 seconds if your computer is offline. We do not keep a history of what you said.
  • Library items — things you explicitly save (diagrams, recipes, playlists) are stored until you delete them, visible only inside your household.
  • Operational logs — errors and delivery events. Logs never contain transcript or library content.

What we don't do

No advertising, no analytics trackers, no selling or sharing data with third parties. The only third parties involved are our infrastructure providers (Cloudflare) and our email provider (Mailgun, for verification emails only).

Isolation

Every household is a separate tenant. Your computer authenticates with a token only your household can mint, and no household can read another's data.

Deletion & questions

Email leif@lalalimited.com to delete your household entirely or ask anything about this policy.
